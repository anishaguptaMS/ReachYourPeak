Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Reach Your Peak Wellness Challenge

How do I get started?

  1. Select "Click here to register as a new user" to register

    1. Select a team captain and name
    2. Check Inbox/Spam folder for "Reach Your Peak" email to verify your email address.
    3. Update your password information.
    4. Login

  2. If your Captain has not created your team yet, please advise them to do so. Once they have, the team will show on the drop down and you will be able to join.

  3. If you are the Captain of your team, go to "My team" to create your team. Once you have done this, other members of your team will be able to join once they have registered and logged in.
